Meet the Fusion Team of 2014!

News & Updates | 0 Comments

The Fusion Film Festival family is happy to welcome over 70 members for the upcoming season of NYU’s premiere student-run film festival! These young men and women are not only the faces behind the scenes of the festival, but also the faces of the future of film, television, and new media and the ever-growing collaboration of the sexes within those fields. From everyone at the Fusion team, we would like to welcome you all to join in our events throughout the year and submit your amazing student work, all leading up to the festival we will work so proudly to present on March 5th – 8th.
