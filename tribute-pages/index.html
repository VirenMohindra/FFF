FESTIVAL DATES: Feb. 26-28, 2015

DEADLINE for Tribute Page Submission & Payment: MON. February 2, 2015

We’re offering you or your company an opportunity to celebrate and support women in the entertainment industry by purchasing a Tribute Page in our Festival Brochure. Our festival is celebrating its 13th birthday and our brochures reach an audience of 3,000 tastemakers, industry leaders, and NYU community. Our brochure is used at every event throughout the three-day festival.

As a business, purchasing a Tribute Page can support our mission of illuminating the next generation of storytellers while having your product reach a sophisticated, creative, and young audience.

As an individual, purchasing a Tribute Page means giving a shoutout to your own FUSION staff member or our inspiring Woman of the Year: Reed Morano!

You write your own message and upload artwork or logos. Our Design Department will make it brochure-ready!
