Sponsorship

THE OPPORTUNITIES

Sponsorship at Fusion Film Festival is a powerful marketing tool with unique packages custom-designed to support your company’s business objectives and align your company’s brand with a festival that targets a key demographic. Outlined below are some of benefits of giving to Fusion – and of course, with each new partnership, we are eager to collaborate to create a custom tailored package that fits your business.

 

Why Sponsor?

THE AUDIENCE

  • Young, contemporary, and media-savvy
  • Cutting edge filmmakers, new media producers, and entertainment thinkers
  • 18 to 40-year-old sophisticated shopper demographic in New York
  • Active media consumers and early adopters
  • Digesting all forms of new media, film, television, games, and social platforms
  • From student filmmakers to industry giants
  • Past guests include film & television luminaries Lucy Alibar, Lynda Obst, Paul Rudd, Lena Dunham, and Penny Marshall

BRANDING OPPORTUNITIES

  • Your logo will be promoted through our Fusion website, Twitter, Instagram and Facebook
  • Your logo will be visible in printed materials
  • Inclusion in Festival press releases
  • Your banner and signage displayed at all events
  • Product and promotion merchandise distribution
  • Recognition announcement at screenings
  • VIP passes to films, programs, and events
  • Invitations to Fusion Festival Wrap Party
  • Access to event photos
  • Product promotion tables offered at event

Sponsorship Levels

PRESENTING SPONSOR (Donation valued at $5000+)

  • Festival “Presented By…”
  • One-minute feature shown ahead of each screening
  • Tribute Page in festival program
  • Promotional product distributed
  • Banner at festival venue, film screenings, and aftery party (sponsor to provide)
  • Recognition during welcoming remarks during festival screenings
  • Logo recognition on press releases, website, and on location at events and parties
  • 10 VIP Passes to all Fusion events including wrap party
  • Category exclusivity

PLATINUM SPONSOR (Donation valued at $2500+)

  • 30-second feature shown ahead of each screening
  • Tribute Page in festival program
  • Promotional product distributed
  • Recognition during welcoming remarks during festival screenings
  • Promotional signage at student competition & after party (sponsor to provide)
  • Logo recognition on press releases, website, on location at events and parties
  • 6 VIP Passes to all Fusion events including wrap party
  • Category exclusivity

GOLD SPONSOR (Donation valued at $1000+)

  • Tribute Page in program
  • Promotional product distributed
  • Recognition during welcoming remarks during festival screenings
  • Logo recognition on press releases, website, on location at events and parties
  • 4 VIP Passes to all Fusion events including wrap party

SILVER SPONSOR (Donation valued at $500-$1,000)

  • Tribute Page in program
  • Promotional product distributed
  • Logo recognition on website and on location at events and parties
  • 4 VIP Passes to all Fusion events including wrap party

BRONZE SPONSOR (Donation valued at under $500)

  • Tribute Page in program
  • Promotional product distributed
  • Logo recognition on website and on location at events and parties
  • 2 VIP Passes to all Fusion events including wrap party
